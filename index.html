Angel Flores

Character Designer and Concept Artist

I am a multimedia artist that combines various mediums in order to transform them into whole interactive experiences. Whether it be trying to create a projected animation onto a welded steel installation, assisting in the conceptual design of a project, or by contributing art assets within a group setting for a video game, or even delving into the aspect of game design itself. I aspire to assist in creating experiences that enable as many human senses as possible. A lot of my work interest is geared towards video game production and development.

I strove to work in this form of medium as video games have always been a part of my life, and the largest influencer for me to become an artist. Fascinated by the act of engagement with the viewer, as well as the aspect of seeing your contribution of work being embedded into something larger, collaborating with other equally skilled individuals..
